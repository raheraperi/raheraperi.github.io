Cultural Blog - Meditation

26 March 2018

Process over Product

I have to admit that my perfectionist ways initially made the rebuild a little overwhelming. In my mind I had a vision of exactly what I wanted to build, and I got extremely frustrated at challenging points of my rebuild when my vision didn't align with what I had the skills to create.

Once I checked in with myself and inquired as to why I was feeling blocked and frustrated, I was able to move through it, embracing all the new things I had learned thus far on my journey. Once I got into a good rhythm with having a tutu with my code, looking at what did and didn't work, and refining, I found that I was way more comfortable and less overwhelmed. It's not about the destination, it's about the journey.

Thoughts on meditation

Ommmmmmmmmmm. Meditation has helped me in many ways. I see it as a form of self-love that helps me stay present rather than getting lost in my thoughts. I would recommend Eckhart Tolle's "The Power of Now" to anyone wanting to explore this idea further.

New learnings

I found the process of mindfulness meditation a useful new learning.

  1. We start with creating an intention for our practice - one that I use commonly is 'lean into challenges with ease'
  2. Next we follow our breath, bringing it focus and attention
  3. If we get distracted, we check in with ourselves and bring our awareness back without judgement
  4. Become aware of our attitude towards ourselves - is this one of love or one of judgement? Maybe this could be your intention for your next practice.

Other musings

Meditation is like magic. Imagine what the world would be like if we were taught meditation as children.
