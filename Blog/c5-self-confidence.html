Cultural Blog - Self Confidence

16 April 2018

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Mindfulness is about noticing. It's not necessarily about hour long meditation or other seemingly intensive spiritual practices, it's about observing our emotions and reactions as they arise. Self-awareness is the key domain of emotional intellgience that help us with self-focused attention and language. When we are aware of our emotions as they arise - whether positive or negative - we can practice mindfulness to choose how we react in these situations and gain control over our emotions. We need self-awareness in order to become aware of our emotions.

Body Scan Experience

A body scan is basically just bringing systematic attention to different parts of the body from top to bottom. Prior to this blog post I had been working on some difficult JavaScript exercises and felt a little tense. When I scanned the body I immediately could feel how much tension I had sitting in my face. When I get stressed I find that I do get tense in the face area. I observed this without judgement and was able to release the tension from my face, and this immediately me feel more relaxed and happy.

"My Emotions are Not Me"

This chapter really reinforced a lot of mindfulness learnings. Self-awareness helps us become more mindful of our emotions and by doing this we realise that our emotions don't define us unless we let them. I think this is a fundamental practice when you're experiencing a lot of stress or the future is uncertain, as it is for a lot of us EDA students. Stress and negative emotion can consume and overwhelm us unless we are kind to ourselves.
